About Me

My name is Anh, and I am an aspiring web/mobile/software developer. I recently graduated from the University of California, San Diego with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

As I proceed with my career, I hope I can use my technical abilities in a meaningful way to help others.

Fun fact: I was fortunate enough to have the opportunity to study abroad at the University College Cork in Ireland during my senior year. I was able to visit many Irish cities (Dublin, Cork, Galway), as well as other cities within Europe (Glasgow, Edinburgh, Barcelona, Amsterdam, and Utrecht). It was, by far, the most rewarding experience I have had in my entire life. Traveling is a passion of mine, and I hope to do more of it as soon as I can.
